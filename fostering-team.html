Fostering Team Wellbeing
Video length: 2:49

Often, well-being is the first thing to slide when other, seemingly more important, things come up (like work). However, well-being isn't a "nice-to-have;" it's a vital part of being a successful human. As a manager, you can foster a culture of well-being on your team by encouraging open discussions about the topic, leading to organic accountability patnerships.

Do the following:

Schedule a team discussion about well-being to discuss how the team can support each other to maximize performance.
