Managing the Flow of Information
Video length: 1:58

Before you dive into the rest of the BillionMinds Program, take a moment to figure out how fast you plan to go through this program. As a manager, you want to go fast enough that you can keep up with your employes, but slow enough that you can make positive changes to your own behaviour, Next, figure out how you will connect with team members who are going through the program at the same time as you. Such as creating a dedicated Slack channel or scheduling "takeaway" meetings for the team.

Do the following in the BillionMinds application:

Create periods on your calender to batch e-mails.
Create a do-not-disturb periond on your calender when notifications are fully switched off.

Determine how you will communicate with your team about the BillionMinds program.
